Manufacturing: paint-booth exhaust heat to 70°C wash water

Illustrative low-lift heat pump recovering booth exhaust to supply parts wash water.

Engineering data

Project background

An automotive supplier exhausted warm booth air while purchasing electric or gas heat for wash lines.

Source / inputs

Source: filtered exhaust air ~28–35°C equivalent. Sink: wash water 65–70°C. Duty: ~350 kW.

Heat pump solution

Air-to-water heat pump with glycol coil recovery and fouling-tolerant exchangers; weekend setback strategy.

COP & savings

Illustrative COP 3.0–3.6; strong when booth runs two shifts.

Investment / ROI notes

Payback often <4 years where electricity is competitive and booth runtime is high.
