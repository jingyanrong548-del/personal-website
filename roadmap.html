Open Thermal AI

Product Roadmap

A staged path from open knowledge and engineering tools to a global industrial thermal AI ecosystem.

  1. Phase 1 · Current

    Knowledge + AI Tools

    Open Knowledge hub, auditable calculators, case library, and AI Thermal Engineer preview (Phase 1 mock / API contract).

  2. Phase 2

    AI Engineering Assistant

    Live AI backend, design reports, and enterprise-oriented engineering assistance grounded in thermal knowledge.

  3. Phase 3

    Digital Twin Simulation Platform

    Cycle and plant-level simulation with digital-twin concepts for industrial heat-pump systems.

  4. Phase 4

    Global Industrial Thermal AI Ecosystem

    Open datasets, shared models, and a worldwide community for industrial thermal intelligence.
